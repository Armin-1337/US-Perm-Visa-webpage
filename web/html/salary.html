US Visa Job Salaries

As to be expected with typical USA average incomes, most salaries range between 60-120k. This means there is no pay discrimination towards immigrants, truly the land of the free. In most cases the US dollar will be stronger than the applicants respective country's currency.

Bachelor degrees are quite lucrative for those seeking work visas, holding masters appears to help the chances of landing higher salary positions, however bachelor holders are still able to hit the same high salary ranges almost equally. Doctorates appear to not be worth the investment as the highest record of doctorate holders fall into the second lowest pay category. For the majority, Having no formal education is not profitable as commonly known, but it won't stop you from entering the USA. Some applicants have even landed very high paying jobs with no formal education.
