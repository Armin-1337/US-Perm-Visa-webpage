Amazon US Visa Statistics


Amazon have accepted a majority of applicants from India in 2016, regardless has a decent spread of applicants from different countries, seeing a much larger portion of Australian applicants compared to other companies.


Amazon's headquarters in Seattle, Washington receives almost all their applicants.


Most of Amazons applicants have at least completed a bachelor’s degree with a majority holding master’s degrees, however that does not stop them from hiring some with no educational background into high paying positions. Amazon along with Google, hold the highest volume of high paying jobs for Visa applicants.
